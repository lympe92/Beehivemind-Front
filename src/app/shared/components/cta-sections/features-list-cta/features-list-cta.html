With our beekeeping software you have all the variables you need to work

Inspection's date

Note the right date that you inspected your apiaries and explore their growth in a long run. After some inspections a complete graph will be available for you. With this you will have an overview about your apiary.

Closed brood & eggs

Use the variables closed brood and eggs separately and gain the advantage of being able to predict the development of the hive in the near future. Furthermore you will be able to know details about the queen's condition.

pollen & honey

Mention the amounts of pollen and honey you find during the inspe­ctions. In this way you will easily be able to find the apiaries or hives which need help or those ready for harvest.

Frames & Population

By using the variables frames and population right, you can be any time aware as to whether your hives need frame addition or removal.

Diseases

Keep notes on the diseases you find in each hive and get rid of them immediately by using the genetic material you have at your disposal.

Info about queen

Every time you see the queen give the right voice command and you will be able to know when was the last time that the hive was prosperous.
